# Hearsay Lyrics: Project Description

**The Pitch:** 
Hearsay Lyrics is an AI-powered KTV companion that generates singable, phonetic English "misheard" lyrics for any Mandarin song. By syncing these English approximations to the original audio and generating immersive background visuals, we enable non-Chinese speakers to seamlessly join karaoke sessions, bridging cultural gaps through music and laughter.

---

## Hackathon Judging Criteria

### **1. Impact Potential (20%)**
*What is the project’s long-term potential for success? Will this project have a long-lasting impact on the industry, world, or any other areas? How useful and substantial is this project beyond the scope of the hackathon?*

**Bridging Cultural Divides:** Hearsay Lyrics breaks down language barriers in social settings. Karaoke (KTV) is a massive global social activity, but non-native speakers are often left out of the fun when foreign language songs are played. By providing instantly singable phonetic translations, we turn an isolating experience into an inclusive one. 

**Beyond KTV:** Beyond the scope of the hackathon, this core technology—phonetic approximation and AI audio-syncing—has massive implications for the **globalization of music**. It offers international fans a deeper connection to artists in the M-Pop, K-Pop, and J-Pop industries, and provides a fun, low-friction entry point for language learners to engage with foreign media without needing to read the native scripts first.

### **2. Live Demo (45%)**
*How well has the team implemented their core idea? Does it work well live? How is it presented?*

**A Flawless, Interactive "Wow" Factor:** Our demo isn't just a backend presentation; it is a fully functional, highly interactive KTV application built for the browser. 
During the live demo, we showcase the raw speed of Gemini by pasting in real lyrics and generating singable English instantly. We prove the robustness of the implementation with features that work flawlessly live:
* **The "Magic" AI Sync:** Watching the UI perfectly track user-uploaded audio in real-time, matching English phrases to Chinese audio without an LRC file.
* **Instant Personalization:** Dragging a slider live to instantly shift the AI's output from "Faithful Translation" to "Hilarious Misheard English," and using AI prompts to instantly refine specific lines.
* **Immersive Visuals:** The demo culminates with a full KTV experience, featuring real-time, AI-generated video and image slideshows that dynamically react to the sentiment of the lyrics.

### **3. Creativity and Originality (35%)**
*Has this concept been seen before? How unique or innovative is this project in its respective industry, field, or area? Does it tackle the problem statements in a unique way?*

**Cross-Language Phonetic Karaoke:** While there are thousands of literal translation apps and language learning tools, Hearsay Lyrics tackles a completely novel problem space: **Cross-language phonetic approximation**. We aren't translating *meaning* (which you can't sing); we are translating *sound and rhythm* into English, which has never been packaged as a deliberate, interactive sing-along bridge.

**Unique Technical Implementation:** We use LLMs not just as chatbots, but as creative rhythmic engines. By combining Gemini's reasoning capabilities to maintain syllable counts, AI audio understanding to map text to raw MP3s, and generative models for background scenery, we've created a completely original, multi-modal pipeline that reimagines how people consume and participate in foreign-language music.
