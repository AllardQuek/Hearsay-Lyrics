# Hearsay Lyrics: 3–4 Minute Live Demo Flow — Refined

## One-line Pitch
Turn Mandarin lyrics into singable English lines that preserve sound, rhythm, and performance.

## Opening Hook (use first 8–12s)
Have you ever wanted to sing a foreign song but the words just won't fit the melody? What if you could sing C-pop in English — and still sound like the original?

Use a 8–12s "wow" moment first: play a pre-cached, performance-ready snippet (Love Confession recommended) so the audience immediately sees the end result.

## Demo Goal
Convince judges that Hearsay makes foreign-language songs performable by anyone by combining fast, line-by-line AI generation, per-line personalization, pinyin/meaning support, and synced playback with optional visuals.

## Core Messaging (3 phrases to repeat)
- We translate singability, not just meaning.
- We preserve sound and rhythm so words map to melody.
- Studio for creation, Presenter for performance.

## Timed Run of Show (3:30 Target)

### 0:00–0:12 | Hook / Instant Wow
- Spoken (presenter): "Have you ever wanted to sing a song in another language but stopped because the words don't fit the melody? Watch this." 
- Action: Play a pre-cached 8–12s clip with Hearsay lines and synced visuals to deliver an immediate "wow." Keep hero + input visible in the UI so judges see context.

### 0:12–0:30 | Quick Promise + Problem
- Spoken: "Translation apps give meaning. Karaoke needs rhythm and phonetic feel. Hearsay gives you singable lines, synced to the music, in seconds."
- Action: Flash the line structure briefly: Chinese → pinyin → hearsay line → meaning.

### 0:30–1:10 | Any-Song Live Generation (show live capability)
- Spoken: "Now watch — I'll paste any Mandarin lyric and generate live, line-by-line." 
- Action: Paste a short, non-catalog lyric (one verse), trigger generation, and show progressive streaming output. Emphasize speed and streaming UX (lines appear as they are generated).

### 1:10–1:40 | Personalization & Refine Loop
- Spoken: "You can tune the style — faithful to playful — and refine lines until they're performance-ready." 
- Action: Move Faithful↔Funny slider, pick a variant, run 'Refine' on one line, and optionally do a quick manual edit.

### 1:40–2:05 | Line-Level Edit → Lock
- Spoken: "A performer can tweak lines per phrase, then lock timing for playback." 
- Action: Edit one line in-place, show diff/variant history, then lock the set for Presenter mode.

### 2:05–2:35 | Presenter Mode + Synced Playback
- Spoken: "Switching to Presenter — now this is a performance-ready track." 
- Action: Switch view to Presenter, start playback on cached or generated audio, let lyric highlight move with music; show pinyin and meaning overlays as optional toggles.

### 2:35–3:05 | Multimedia Wow & Social Moment
- Spoken: "We can add visuals, stage cues, and invite people to sing along — or export a shareable clip." 
- Action: Toggle AI visual backdrop, optionally show generated short video clip or storyboard; invite an audience member (or play a backing track) for a quick sing-along highlight.

### 3:05–3:30 | Close & Call to Action
- Spoken: "Hearsay turns songs into shared performances. It makes C-pop participation inclusive, playful, and production-ready. Try it with your song — or connect via our API to integrate it into any karaoke app." 
- Action: Show sign-up/next-steps slide and one-sentence roadmap (duet mode, mobile, scoring).

### Differentiator — Why We're Unique
- Blend of entertainment + education: Hearsay is performance-first (singability, timing, stage-ready output) while simultaneously supporting learning (pinyin, phonetic anchors, meaning). This dual focus unlocks both casual karaoke users and learners.
- Performance-first models: we optimize outputs for syllable count, stress, and singability rather than literal fidelity, producing lines that map naturally to melodies.
- Streaming, per-line generation + timing lock: judges see instant feedback and deterministic playback for live performance — a UX advantage over batch translators or static lyric apps.
- Integration & export: sync-ready Presenter mode, AI visual backdrops, short video export, and an API/SDK make Hearsay embeddable across KTVs, apps, and creator tools.
- Defensibility: proprietary phonetic alignment techniques, curated singability datasets, and UX that couples editing + locked timing make our product hard to replicate quickly.

### Business, GTM & Monetization (judge-facing summary)
- Target customers: KTV operators / karaoke app platforms, language-learners & learners-as-creators, music creators/publishers, and social/live-stream platforms.
- GTM channels: pilot with KTV chains and leading karaoke apps; creator & influencer seeding (TikTok/Kwai); embed SDK into streaming and language platforms; campus & community campaigns to drive viral challenges.
- Revenue model: B2B licensing (per-device or SaaS seat) for KTVs and apps, API/credit model for integrators, consumer subscriptions for premium features (exports, high-quality audio/video, duet mode), and microtransactions/packs for premium lines/visuals. Offer revenue-share deals with rights-holders for official song catalogs.
- Early metrics to show judges: pilot conversion rate, songs transformed, DAU/MAU retention, avg revenue per user (ARPU), and CLTV. For demos, call out a realistic pilot target (e.g., 3 KTV pilots, 10k transformed songs) to frame traction potential.
- Ask / next-step pitch: seek pilot partners (KTV chains or karaoke apps), intros to music publishers, and strategic advisors/connections. Optionally request a small pilot budget or introductions rather than an immediate large investment.
